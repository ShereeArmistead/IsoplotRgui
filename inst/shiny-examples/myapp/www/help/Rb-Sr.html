Rb87/Sr86: 87Rb/86Sr-ratios.
s[Rb87/Sr86]: Analytical uncertainties of the 87Rb/86Sr-ratios.
Sr87/Sr86: Measured 87Sr/86Sr-ratios.
s[Sr87/Sr86]: Analytical uncertainties of the 87Sr/86Sr-ratios.
(rho): Error correlations between the 87Rb/86Sr and 87Sr/86Sr-ratios (optional).
Rb: Rb concentration, in ppm.
s[Rb]: Analytical uncertainty of the Rb concentration, in ppm.
Sr: Sr concentration, in ppm.
s[Sr]: Analytical uncertainty of the Sr concentration, in ppm.
87/86: Measured 87Sr/86Sr-ratios.
s[87/86]: Analytical uncertainties of the 87Sr/86Sr-ratios.
(C): Optional variable to be used as fill colours in isochron and radial plots.
(omit): Optional variable used to hide or ignore aliquots. Aliquots marked as (lowercase) 'x' are plotted (except on KDEs and CADs) but ignored for numerical analysis; Aliquots marked as (uppercase) 'X' are removed from both plots and calculations.

IsoplotR uses these data to calculate the 87Rb/86Sr-ratios and the correlation between the 87Sr/86Sr- and the 87Rb/86Sr-uncertainties.
