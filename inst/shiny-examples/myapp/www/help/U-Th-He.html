He, U, Th, Sm: Measured He, U, Th and (optionally) Sm abundance or concentration, in any kind of atomic units (fmol, mol/g, atoms, ...).
s[He], s[U], s[Th], s[Sm]: Standard errors of the He, U, Th and (optionally) Sm measurements.
(C): Optional variable to be used as fill colours in radial plots, ternary diagrams and logratio plots.
(omit): Optional variable used to hide or ignore aliquots. Aliquots marked as '1' or 'x' are plotted (except on KDEs and CADs) but ignored for numerical analysis; Aliquots marked as '2' or 'X' (uppercase 'x') are removed from both plots and calculations.
