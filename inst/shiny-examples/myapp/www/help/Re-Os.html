Input:

Re: Re concentration, in ppm.
s[Re]: Analytical uncertainty of the Re concentration, in ppm.
Os: Os concentration, in ppm.
s[Os]: Analytical uncertainty of the Os concentration, in ppm.
187/188: Measured 187Os/188Os-ratios.
s[187/188]: Analytical uncertainties of the 187Os/188Os-ratios.

IsoplotR uses these data to calculate the 187Re/188Os-ratios and the correlation between the 187Os/188Os- and the 187Re/188Os-uncertainties.
