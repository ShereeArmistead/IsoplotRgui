Input:

39/40: Measured 39Ar/40Ar-ratios.
s[39/40]: Standard error of the 39Ar/40Ar-ratios.
36/40: Measured 36Ar/40Ar-ratios.
s[36/40]: Standard error of the 36Ar/40Ar-ratios.
39/36: Measured 39Ar/36Ar-ratios.
s[39/36]: Standard error of the 39Ar/36Ar-ratios.
40/36: Measured 40Ar/36Ar-ratios.
s[40/36]: Standard error of the 40Ar/36Ar-ratios.
rho: Error correlation between the 40Ar/36Ar- and 39Ar/36Ar-ratios.
(rho): Error correlation between the 36Ar/40Ar- and 39Ar/40Ar-ratios (optional).
(39): Amount of 39Ar in each heating step, which will be normalised to form the X-axis of the age spectrum (optional).

IsoplotR uses the redundancy of the three ratios to calculate the correlation between their respective analytical uncertainties.
