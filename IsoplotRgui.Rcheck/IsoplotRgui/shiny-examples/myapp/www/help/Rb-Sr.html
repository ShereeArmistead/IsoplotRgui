Input:

Rb: Rb concentration, in ppm.
s[Rb]: Analytical uncertainty of the Rb concentration, in ppm.
Sr: Sr concentration, in ppm.
s[Sr]: Analytical uncertainty of the Sr concentration, in ppm.
87/86: Measured 87Sr/86Sr-ratios.
s[87/86]: Analytical uncertainties of the 87Sr/86Sr-ratios.

IsoplotR uses these data to calculate the 87Rb/86Sr-ratios and the correlation between the 87Sr/86Sr- and the 87Rb/86Sr-uncertainties.
